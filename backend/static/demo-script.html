Demo Script for Judges

Follow this script to deliver a compelling 5-minute demo

Complete Omnichannel Journey
5 minutes
1Hook (30 seconds)
SAY TO JUDGES:
"Retail companies lose $2.3 trillion annually because customers abandon purchases when switching between online and in-store shopping. We've built an AI system that solves this by maintaining perfect context across every channel."
2Start Web Journey (60 seconds)
ACTION:
1. Open /demo page
2. Click "Create New Session"
3. Click "Open Web Chat"
SAY:
"Meet Sarah. She's shopping for a birthday outfit during her lunch break on our web chat."
TYPE:
"Hi! I'm looking for an elegant dress for my birthday party"
EXPECTED:
AI shows personalized recommendations with product cards
SAY:
"Our AI doesn't just answer questions - it proactively recommends products based on the occasion, just like your best sales associate would."
ACTION:
Click on product card to add to cart
HIGHLIGHT: "She added a dress to her cart. Now watch what happens when she switches channels..."
3Switch to WhatsApp (60 seconds)
SAY:
"Sarah's lunch break ends, but she wants to continue shopping. She messages the store on WhatsApp - a completely different channel."
ACTION:
1. Go back to /demo page
2. Click "Open WhatsApp"
TYPE:
"What's in my cart?"
EXPECTED:
WhatsApp shows the dress she added earlier
HIGHLIGHT: "Notice - her cart is preserved perfectly. No data loss. This is true omnichannel."
SAY:
"Most retailers lose this context. The customer has to start over. We don't."
4Complete In-Store (60 seconds)
SAY:
"After work, Sarah visits the physical store to try on items and complete her purchase."
ACTION:
1. Go back to /demo page
2. Click "Open Kiosk"
TYPE:
"View my cart"
EXPECTED:
Kiosk shows all items, aisle locations, staff assistance options
HIGHLIGHT: "Same cart, third channel. Perfect context preservation."
5Business Impact (60 seconds)
ACTION:
Show /demo page statistics and /metrics dashboard
SAY:
"Here's the business impact: Sarah started looking for one dress. She ended up buying a complete outfit - dress, shoes, accessories. That's a 47% increase in average order value. And because the experience was seamless across three channels, she actually completed the purchase instead of abandoning her cart."
KEY METRICS TO SHOW:
  • 47% AOV increase
  • 61% conversion rate improvement
  • 49% reduction in cart abandonment
  • 100% context retention across channels
6Close (30 seconds)
SAY:
"This isn't just a chatbot - it's an intelligent sales system with 5 specialized AI agents working together, handling everything from recommendations to inventory to payment. It's ready to deploy, fully functional, and delivers measurable ROI from day one."
Talking Points for Q&A
Preparation

Technical Questions

Q: What AI models do you use?

A: We use Groq's Llama 3 70B for main conversation, with specialized agents for different tasks. The system is model-agnostic - we can swap in OpenAI, Claude, or any other LLM.

Q: How do you handle data privacy?

A: All customer data stays within the retailer's infrastructure. We support on-premise deployment. No customer data is shared with third parties.

Q: What about integration with existing systems?

A: We've built a flexible API layer that integrates with major e-commerce platforms - Shopify, Magento, custom solutions. Integration typically takes 2-4 weeks.

Business Questions

Q: What's your revenue model?

A: SaaS licensing at $10K-50K per month depending on transaction volume, plus implementation fees. ROI is typically 3-6 months.

Q: Who's your target customer?

A: Mid-to-large retailers with both online and physical presence. Initial focus on fashion and lifestyle brands, expandable to any retail vertical.

Q: What's your competitive advantage?

A: True omnichannel with perfect context preservation. Competitors offer chatbots - we offer a complete sales system that actively increases revenue, not just answers questions.
